Prevention

Sandmining

Sandmining is not allowed, if a player sandmines the military may be deployed and if itâ€™s severe the criminal may be given a fine of up to 1g per chunk affected. If the fine is not paid back within 2 weeks the amount fined will go up each week by 1G per chunk

Scams

Failing to follow through with a deal without refunding or attempting to trade for an unreasonable price with the intention of making an unfair profit will result in a fine of up to 16G plus 50% of the value of the scam; the scammer must refund and give compensation of 16G plus 25% of the scam's value or what a judge deems appropriate.

Trapping

If a player traps a citizen of Sudan, they will be required to give the victim their stuff back. If they have a high ranking position such as military member or minister in the nation it may be removed. A fine of up to 10g may be issued.

Fines

If a citizen doesn't pay a fine, they won't be able to run for a Ministerial position or join the Military until that fine is payed off. If a fine is not payed back after 2 weeks, the amount fined will go up by 1G each week until it's payed back. If a member of the Military or a person who is in a Ministerial position has a fine that they havent paid back after 2 weeks they will be removed from their position.
