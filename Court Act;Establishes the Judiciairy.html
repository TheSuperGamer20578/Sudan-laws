Judges

A judge is someone who is responseble for:

  • Knowing about laws.
  • Resolving legal disputes.
  • Assisting Ministers.
  • Enforcing laws.
  • Maintaining precedent.

Appointment

A judge may be appointed or unappointed at any time by the ministry.

Royal prerogative

If the Royalty agrees that a judge has committed misconduct or is causing harm they may be unappointed.

Resignation

A judge may not resign, they must instead ask the Ministry to unappoint them.

Ministers

Someone who is directly a Minister cannot become a Judge.
Someone who is a Judge cannot directly become a Minister.
