Soldiers

Joining

For a citizen to be eligible to join the Sudanese military they must've been in the nation for over 5 days and be online at least twice in that time. If a citizen meets all the requirements to join the military, they may ask the leader of the military to join, then the leader of the the military will bring them to the next training session; at the next training session they will battle the leader of the military three times with all the trainers who are present at that training session watching, afterwards the trainers will decide if they want to train that citizen; if multiple trainers want to train the citizen, the citizen will choose a trainer. If the citizen gets a trainer they will be part of the military and get the equipment stated in Section 1.3.

Maintaining military member status

To maintain the status of a military member, a member of the military must be active at least once per week on average. If a military member has not logged on for 4 weeks they will be removed from the military and lose their military ranks.

Equipment

Once a citizen joins the military, they will be given either fully enchanted gear or some netherite ingots to upgrade their current gear. A military member may request extra gear such as a bow or crossbow if necessary.

Equipment loss

If a military member loses or breaks their equipment they may request new gear from the leader of the military, however, they will have to cover half the cost. If a military member loses their gear 3 times within a week, they will either be removed from the military or lose their highest rank.

Ranks

The Leader

The person with this rank is the leader of the military. They are appointed by the Minister of Defence. They operate the military and monitor who joins it. They have the power to remove people from the military.

Trainer

The people with this rank are in charge of training the rest of the military. To earn this rank they must prove to the leader that they are very knowledgeable, active and trustworthy.

soldier

Anyone who is active and meets the requirements stated in Section 1.1 is eligible for this rank and will help in battles and do regular training with trainers, if the trainers find that a soldier isn’t good enough at PVP they will have their soldier rank removed. Every soldier will have their own assigned trainer.

Training sessions

The Leader of the military is to organize weekly training sessions on the training server with the IP "SudanEMC.aternos.me". Trainers may choose to attend the training session with their soldiers. If the training server is unavailable, the leader of the military may choose to skip that training session or use another server after consulting with the Minister of Defence. During a training session trainers are responsible for training their soldiers, however, the leader of the military may choose to have training activities involving the whole military.

Leaving

If a military member feels they don’t want to be in the military anymore, they may ask to be removed without any punishment. If a soldier leaves the nation or fails to meet the requirements, they will be removed from the military.
