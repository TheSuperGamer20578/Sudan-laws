Requirements

To Run

To run for either of the two positions of the Consulate, the player must be in a nation within the Tanzanian Empire, and the nation must be in the SMRF agreement, not hostile to Sudan, and approved by either king of Sudan to run.

Power

Voting

Supreme Consul

The Supreme Consul is to be supreme over the Grand Consul and has the ability to veto anything the Grand Consul proposes up to five times between each election session. The Supreme Consul may also do something called a Cogo te ad suffragium mecum ten times, or, "I force you to vote with me," shortened to Ctasm, which forces the Grand Consul's vote (in to agree with the Supreme Consul's). The Supreme Consul is to have one vote in the Ministry, and only one person is to obtain the seat of Supreme Consul.

Grand Consul

The Grand Consul is to have one vote in the Ministry and the ability to transfer the Grand Consul's voting power in the ministry, which is one vote, to the Supreme Consul for up to one week at a time up to three times each term.

Elections

The Grand and Supreme Consuls are to be elected by majority by the electorate within Sudan.

Term Length

The Grand and Supreme Consuls' term lengths are to be the same as any other minister, one month.
